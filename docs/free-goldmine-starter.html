Your first World Cup-season money idea map.

Your first workflow

  1. Pick one lane and one platform.
  2. Collect 10 current football questions or debates.
  3. Write 5 hooks and produce 3 posts in one format.
  4. Track saves, comments, profile clicks, and link clicks.

If the same format produces two real signals, repeat it. If not, change the hook, not the whole idea.
