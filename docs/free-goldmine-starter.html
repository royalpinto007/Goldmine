One simple AI workflow

Turn one topic into four assets.

This is the starter workflow to run before buying tools or building automation.

Input

  • Topic: a player debate, match question, or fan problem.
  • Audience: casual fans, fantasy players, training fans, or creators.
  • Format: reel, carousel, poll, or newsletter.

Prompt

Give me 5 football content angles for [topic]. For each angle, include a hook, a 30-second script, one visual idea, one caption, and one safe monetization path. Avoid copyrighted clips and fake income claims.

7-day starter path

Your first test week.

Days 1 to 3

  • Pick one idea lane.
  • Write 10 hooks.
  • Publish 3 simple posts in the same format.
  • Save comments and questions.

Days 4 to 7

  • Turn the best post into a second format.
  • Create one useful link destination.
  • Add a small CTA.
  • Decide whether to repeat, refine, or stop.
