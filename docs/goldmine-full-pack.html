7-day sprint and rules

Run this before overbuilding.

Days 1 to 3

  • Choose one playbook.
  • Write 10 hooks.
  • Publish 3 posts in one format.
  • Track saves, comments, shares, and profile clicks.

Days 4 to 7

  • Remix the best post into a second format.
  • Create one useful link destination.
  • Test one CTA.
  • Write down what strangers actually responded to.

Good paths

  • Affiliate links to genuinely useful tools or products.
  • Small digital products like prompt packs, calendars, templates, and guides.
  • Services for creators or fan pages after showing proof.
  • Newsletters and community offers once trust exists.

Avoid

  • Fake income screenshots.
  • Unauthorized player images or copyrighted match clips.
  • Pretending players endorse your product.
  • Selling generic AI slop with no taste or verification.
A good idea is specific enough to post today, useful enough to save, and honest enough to build trust.
